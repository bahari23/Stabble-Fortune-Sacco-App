Member Deposits

  • Minimum monthly deposit: Ksh 1,000
  • Deposits are fully refundable on membership termination
  • Higher deposits give you greater borrowing power
  • Deposits used as collateral for borrowing
  • Refundable 60 days after official notice, subject to debt clearance

Share Capital

  • Required to earn dividends: Ksh 10,000 total
  • Each share = Ksh 20  ·  Minimum 500 shares
  • Payable in bits of Ksh 1,000/month or as a lump sum
  • Dividends paid at year-end
  • Maximum holding: 20% of total member share capital
  • Share capital is transferable (not refundable) on exit
